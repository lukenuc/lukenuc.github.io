About

Graduate researcher focused on optimal control and state estimation as applied to automotive and battery systems. I work in the Electrified and Intelligent Systems Automation (ELISA) Lab at Oakland University. Current research interests include low-compute data-driven predictive control and dense extended Kalman filtering for state-of-charge estimation.
